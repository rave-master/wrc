Sustainable Development Goals

Our wildlife rescue center directly contributes to achieving the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals:

SDG 15: Life on Land

Protecting terrestrial ecosystems, combating desertification, and halting biodiversity loss through wildlife rescue and rehabilitation.

SDG 14: Life Below Water

Conserving marine and coastal ecosystems, including rescue of marine wildlife and habitat protection.

SDG 17: Partnerships

Collaborating with local communities, government agencies, and international organizations for wildlife conservation.

SDG 4: Quality Education

Promoting environmental education and awareness about wildlife conservation and sustainable practices.

SDG 3: Good Health

Ensuring healthy ecosystems that support both wildlife and human well-being through disease prevention and monitoring.

SDG 11: Sustainable Cities

Creating sustainable urban-wildlife interfaces and promoting coexistence in developing communities.
